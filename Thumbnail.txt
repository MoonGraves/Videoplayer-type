What is ffmpeg?
it's a free and open-source software tool for editing video or image/pictures. 
Making file smaller keep quality same as possible for example 1.4G to shrinks around 500Mt file.
It can be handling multiple video, audio and other multimedia files and streams.
At its core is the FFmpeg program itself, designed for command-line-based processing of video and audio files. 
It is widely used for format transcoding, basic editing (trimming and concatenation), video scaling, video post-production effects and standards compliance (SMPTE, ITU).

For examples:
- video files 2k to 1280x720
- remove/add sounds of video behind scene
- editing video filters of yuv, hue or YCbCr and ect color types.
- speed up or slow up of video, if speed up turn to timelapse video

FFmpeg is usually used by .exe file or cmd
there is more documentation about ffmpeg of this website: https://ffmpeg.org/ffmpeg.html & https://ffmpeg.org/documentation.html
